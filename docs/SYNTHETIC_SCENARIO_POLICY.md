# Synthetic curated scenario policy

The current curated scenarios use synthetic CVE-like identifiers and deterministic infrastructure contexts. They are intended for method development, reproducibility, and pipeline validation.

## Current interpretation

- CVE identifiers such as CVE-2025-0001 are placeholders unless explicitly replaced by real CVE records.
- Scenario names, assets, and vulnerability rows are curated research fixtures.
- Results should be reported as prototype deterministic environmental scoring outputs, not as claims about real-world CVE risk.

## Article language requirement

The manuscript should describe the current data as synthetic curated scenarios unless real CVE records are later introduced with citations and source evidence.
